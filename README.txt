Collect consents with our cookie pop-up to build trust and show your customers that you respect their privacy. 

== Description ==

Our cookie consent solution is built and kept up to date in partnership with leading law firms around the world. 

Get started with our free cookie consent solution that looks good and is easy to implement. Use Legal Monster to collect and record consent for all cookies used on your site.

== Installation ==

1. Upload and unzip `legalmonster.zip` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Go to `Settings` -> `Legal Monster Cookie` and paste your Cookie widget code.
